# I. Basic pharmacology of antihypertensive agents
![[抗高血压药的分类.png|500]]
### 1. Diuretics 
[[13-1 利尿药 Diuretic Agents]]
drugs that alter sodium & water balance, depleting the sodium and reducing blood volume, and perhaps by other mechanisms
1. 高效利尿药，速尿（*布美他尼*）-排钾利尿
2. 中效利尿药，*氢氯噻嗪*-排钾利尿
3. 醛固酮受体阻断剂（*螺内酯*）-保钾利尿
4. Na+通道阻断剂（*阿米洛利*）-保钾利尿

###### 1. Pharmacological roles
1. 利尿作用
	- 早期-降低血容量及心输出量
	- 晚期-降低外周血管阻力
2. 非利尿作用
	- 直接扩血管作用（吲达帕胺）
	- 阿米洛利-钠通道阻断剂，阻断平滑肌对收缩刺激的反应
###### 2. 临床应用
- 单独使用--轻/中度高血压；
- 与交感及扩血管药物合用
- **Dosing considerations**
	- threshold amount of body sodium depletion may be sufficient for anti-hypertensive efficacy.
	- The blood pressure response to loop diuretics continues to increase at doses many times greater than the usual therapeutic dose.
###### 3. 不良反应
- *Disturbance of electrolytes（电解质紊乱）*: Hypokalemia, Hyponatremia, Hypomagnesemia, hypochloremia. Hypercalcemia for thiazide, and hypocalcemia for furosemide (renal stone)
- Impair glucose tolerance, induce *hyperglycemia* ;
- Increase serum lipid concentrations, induce *hyperlipidemia*;
- *Hyperuricemia*, precipitate gout;

###### ==Propranolol==
1. mechanisms & sites of action: nonselective--block in brain, kidney and heart etc.
2. clinical uses:
	1. Hypertension: all kinds
		- more effective in young than elderly
		- useful in treating coexisting conditions
	2. other uses: angina pectoris, arrhythmias, heart failure
3. side effects:
	1. Bradycardia or cardiac conduction disease (over inhibition)
	2. Asthma (Non-selective)
	3. Peripheral vascular insufficiency
	4. Diabetes
	5. *Withdrawal syndrome* after prolonged regular use

#####  α1 blockers
###### ==Prazosin 哌唑嗪== 
1. mechanisms & sites of action
	- Reduce arterial pressure by dilating both resistance and capacitance vessels.
	- Without affects on NE release (α2 function)
	- blood pressure is reduced more in the upright than in the supine position.
	- Retention of salt and water occurs.
	- The drugs are more effective when used in combination with other agents, such as a β-blocker and a diuretic, than when used alone. **By reflex**
2. Pharmacokinetics & Dosage
	- Although long-term treatment with these α-blockers causes *relatively little postural hypotension（体位性低血压）*, it appears *first-dose phenomenon* — precipitous drop in standing blood pressure develops in a number of patients shortly after the first dose is absorbed.

##### ==Nitroprusside 硝普纳==
- parenteral 肠道外给药
- **hypertensive emergencies**
- Dilates **both arterial and venous vessels,** resulting in reduced peripheral vascular resistance and venous return
- Powerful parenterally administered vasodilator
- The mechanism: **produce NO** and stimulate guanylyl cyclase (GC), thus increase cGMP, which relaxes vascular smooth muscle.
- Used in patients with **hypertensive emergencies or severe heart failure**.
- ***In the absence of heart failure***, blood pressure decreases, owing to decreased vascular resistance, while cardiac output does not change or decreases slightly.
- ***In patients with heart failure*** and low cardiac output, output often increases owing to afterload reduction.

---
#### Arterial

##### ==Minoxidil 米诺地尔==
- potassium channel openers (PCOs), vasodilating
- **Dilates arterioles but not veins.**
- Can be used in patients with *renal failure* and severe hypertension, who do not respond well to hydralazine.
- Minoxidil is associated with *reflex sympathetic stimulation* and sodium and fluid retention. Minoxidil must be used in combination with β blocker, α blocker and a loop diuretic.、
- Adverse effects: Headache, sweating, and hirsutism （ 多 毛 症 ） , which is particularly bothersome in women
---
##### ==Hydralazine 肼屈嗪==
- long-term outpatient therapy of hypertension
- **Dilates arterioles but not veins**.
- Disadvantage: tolerance to hypertensive effects developed rapidly.
- Combination therapy are now suggested. Especially in severe hypertension.
- **Bioavailability is low (averaging 25%)** because of the first pass metabolism and variable among individuals. The half- life of hydralazine ranges from 2 to 4 hours.
- In patients with *ischemic heart disease*, reflex tachycardia and sympathetic stimulation may provoke angina or ischemic arrhythmias.【adverse effects: infrequent but severe】
---
##### Fenoldopam（菲诺多泮） 
- an agonist of **dopamine D1 receptors,** resulting in dilation of peripheral arteries and natriuresis（排钠）.
- increases intraocular pressure and should be avoided in patients with glaucoma（青光眼）.
- Its *half-life is 10 minutes*. The drug is administered by continuous intravenous infusion.

# II. Clinical pharmacology of antihypertensive agents

## *Monotherapy Vs. Polypharmacy in Hypertension*

- 首选对靶器官有保护作用的药物
- **Mild:** diuretics==D==, β blockers==B==, ACEI/ARB==A==, calcium channel blockers==C==, α1 blockers, single drug.
- **Moderate**: combined with two of above drugs
- **Severe**: adding centrally acting drugs or vasodilators to the two combined drugs
---
## *Hypertension emergency*

- Vasodilators (nitroprusside sodium, diazoxide)
- Labetalol
- Loop diuretics
