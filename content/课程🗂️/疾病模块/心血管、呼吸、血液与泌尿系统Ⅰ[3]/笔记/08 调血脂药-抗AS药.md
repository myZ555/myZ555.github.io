1. 防治脂质代谢障碍
2. 稳定斑块

- The physiologic role of cholesterol
	- Component of all cell membranes
	- Precursor of other steroids (cortisol糖皮质激素, progesterone孕酮, estrogen雌激素, etal)
- Serum lipid: 甘油三酯TG，胆固醇TC，磷脂，游离脂肪酸
- ![400](Pasted%20image%2020250314095915.png)
- Lipid metabolism:
- ![400](Pasted%20image%2020250314095937.png)
- Risk factors for CHD: **high LDL and low HDL**
	- ==LDL (low density lipoprotein)==
		- "bad cholesterol"
		- Associated with increased heart disease
		- Major carrier of cholesterol in the blood
		- Role: transport cholesterol to peripheral tissues
		- The LDL receptor is central to cholesterol homeostasis
	- ==HDL (high density lipoprotein)==
		- Secreted by the liver and intestine
		- Lipids of HDL come from CM and VLDL during lipolysis  or acquires from peripheral tissued
		- Role: maintain the cholesterol homeostasis
		- Low HDL is an independent risk factor for CHD
- Simple classification of hyperlipidemias: LDL↑, HDL↓

#### 1. 调血脂药

##### 1. HMG-CoA Reductase inhibitors (statins, 他汀类)
[](Pharmacology%20of%20BMS1_药理_zsy_net.pdf#page=22&selection=277,0,282,0|Pharmacology%20of%20BMS1_药理_zsy_net,%20页面%2022)
- HMG-CoA, 羟甲基戊二酸单酰辅酶A。抑制胆固醇从头合成途径
- ![500](Pasted%20image%2020250314100428.png)
	- 结构与HMG-CoA相似，可竞争HMG-CoA 还原酶
	- 由于首过效应，主要作用于肝脏
	- 增加high-affinity LDL receptors，增加LDL代谢
	- 抑制VLDL的合成和释放，thus reducing plasma LDL
- 药理学作用：
	- lipid lowing 降脂作用
	- non-lipid lowing effects（inhibit the foam cell and stabilize plaque）
- 临床应用
	1. 调节血脂
	2. 肾病综合征
	3. Prevention of acute cardiocerebrovascular attack
	4. Retenning after percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty抑制血管成形术后再狭窄
	5. Osteoporosis 骨质疏松症
	6. Rejection after organ transplantation
- 剂量效应：*双重反馈*：外界吸收增加+基因反馈性表达增加，剂量升高而疗效则增加不显著
- 不良反应：胃肠道/肝毒性/肌病

##### 2. cholesterol absorption inhibitors （CAIs, 胆固醇吸收抑制药）
- **Ezetimibe** 依折麦布
1. Mechanisms: blocks *cholesterol absorption at the intestinal brush border*
	- No effect on absorption of lipid-soluble vitamins
	- No effect on bile acid absorption
2. Indications: high LDL
3. 药理学特点：最小系统暴露（仅作用于小肠绒毛细胞刷状缘，非常局限）
4. 临床应用：耐受度好
##### 3. Bile acid-binding resins (RESINS 胆酸结合树脂)
1. **Cholestyramine考来烯胺，消胆胺, colestipol考来替泊，降胆宁, colesevelam 考来维伦**
2. Mechanisms: 结合胃肠道中的胆汁酸，抑制肝肠循环，增加粪便排出
	1. LDL 受体代偿性↑（外源性吸收减少，肝内受体代偿性表达）
	2. HMG-CoA还原酶继发性活性增加
3. Efficacy: LDL↓
4. 注意：可用于有胆囊症状的患者（胆汁淤积症）；许多药物可能与其发生相互作用（洋地黄苷）
5. 不良反应：
	1. 大剂量使用影响脂溶性维生素吸收
	2. 便秘
##### 4. Proprotein convertase subtilisin / kexin type 9 inhibitor PCSK9 inhibitor 前蛋白转化酶枯草溶菌酶9抑制剂

1. PCSK9, a secretory serine protease 分泌型丝氨酸蛋白酶
2. Binds to LDL receptor, and promote LDL receptor internalization for degradation  结合LDL受体促进其内吞和降解
##### 5. Acyl-coenzyme A cholesterol acyltransferase inhibitor (ACAT inhibitor）
1. ACAT:
	1. Enhance VLDL synthesis in liver
	2. Enhance cholesterol accumulation in vascular wall
	3. Increase cholesterol absorption in intestine
	4. Enhance the formation of macrophages and foam cells
2. Melinamide 甲亚油酰胺
##### 6. Fibric acid derivatives (fibrates) 贝特类
1. **Gemfibrozil 吉非贝齐 and fenofibrate 非诺贝特**
2. 增加HDL、降低TG
3. 不良反应：皮疹、胃肠道反应；胆结石；他汀类联合使用增加肝病风险（严重：横纹肌溶解）
##### 7. 烟酸类
1. **阿昔莫司**
	1. 抑制肝脏VLDL/LDL的合成，增加VLDL清除
	2. TC ↓ 25%, LDL ↑ 10-25%, HDL ↑  10-40%, TG ↓  20-50%
2. 不良反应：潮红、升高血糖
