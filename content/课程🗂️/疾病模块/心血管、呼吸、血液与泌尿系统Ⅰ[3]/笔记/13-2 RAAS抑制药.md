# 1. Renin inhibitor
###### Aliskiren，阿利吉仑

- Bioavailability 2.5%，iv infusion, Vd 135L，Binding 47-51%
- 单独或与其他降压药物合用治疗高血压。
- 不良反应有腹泻、头痛、头晕、 乏力、咳嗽等。
# 2. Angiotensin converting enzyme inhibitors (ACEI)

==Bradykinin 缓激肽== 
- **Stimulation of endothelial NO formation—— Vasodilatation**
- Stimulation of prostaglandin (PG) formation
- Inhibition of sodium and water reabsorption (collecting tubules)
- Increase in vascular permeability
- Contraction of visceral smooth muscle
- Role in nociception
**==Pharmacokinetics**==
Enalapril: $t_{1/2}$ ~11 hours;  Lisinopril : $t_{1/2}$~ 12 hours
The elimination is different.
Some are prodrugs(前药), enalapril and fosinopril
There are multiple binding sites for ACEI

**==Pharmacological effects==**
- Inhibition the production of AngII 
- Increase the accumulation of bradykinin 
- Protect the function of endothelia cells 
- Protect the cardiomyocyte
- Increase the sensitivity to insulin

**==Clinical Usage==**
- hypertension
- chronic heart failure and myocardia infarction
- kidney diseases
- arrhythmia

**==Adverse effects==**
- Severe **hypotension** (esp. hypovolemic due to diuretics, salt restriction, or gastrointestinal fluid loss). **First dose phenomenon**
- **Acute renal failure** (particularly in patients with bilateral renal artery stenosis or stenosis of the renal artery of a solitary kidney),
- **Hyperkalemia**, the decrease of aldosterone release
- **Dry cough** and angioedema
- Hypoglycemia
- **MAY** induce lung cancers

###### Captopril 卡托普利
- The 1 st ACEI
- bioavailability 75%, food affects its absorption
- t 1/2 2 hr, 40-50% prototype excreted by kidney
- -SH has free radical scavenging effects
- may induce rash, abnormal taste, eosinophilia ( 嗜酸性粒细胞增多), neutropenia .
###### Enalapril 依那普利
- Prototype
- bioavailability 75%, food affects its absorption
- t max 4-6 hr, effects continue 24 hr
- t 1/2 11 hr, excreted through kidney
###### Lisinopril 赖诺普利
- bioavailability 25%, <10% protein binding
-  t max ~7 hr, t 1/2 12 hr, effects continue 24 hr
-  prototype excreted by kidney
###### Benazepril 贝那普利→ 贝那普利酸
- Prodrug, t max 4 hr, effects continue 24 hr
- t 1/2 10-11 hr
- Metabolized by liver, partly excreted by liver
- Safe with kidney or liver dysfunction
###### Fosinopril 福辛普利 → 福辛普利酸
- Prodrug with POO-
- Bioavailability 36%, > 95% protein binding
- t max 3-6 hr, effects continue 24 hr
- t 1/2 12 hr
- More efficient with ACE in brain and heart, less for ACE in kidney 
- Excreted by both kidney and liver
- Secretion in milk

# 3. Angiotensin II receptor blocker, ARB
- No effect on bradykinin metabolism
- Complete blocking the effects of AngII on AT1
- May increase AngII by reflex, which acts on AT2
- The adverse effects are similar to those described for ACE inhibitors, including the hazard of use during pregnancy.
- Cough and angioedema can occur but are much less common.
- 安全且耐受性好，尤其适用于高血压伴左心室肥厚、心力衰竭、 糖尿病肾病、冠心病、微量白蛋白尿或蛋白尿患者，以及不能 耐受ACE 抑制药的患者，并可预防心房颤动

###### Losartan 氯沙坦
- Bioavailability 33%, 14% metabolized to EXP3174
-  Mostly metabolized by P450
- Selective to AT1, EXP3174 为氯沙坦的活性代谢物，其阻断 AT1 受体的作用比氯沙坦强10～40 倍。
- 用于高血压。在肾脏还有促进尿酸排泄作用，可用于高血压 合并尿酸血症的治疗。长期用药可抑制左室心肌肥厚和血管 壁增厚，可用于慢性心力衰竭合并高血压的治疗。对糖尿病 合并肾功能不全患者也有保护作用。
- Not for pregnant women, lactating women, kidney artery stenosis
- May cause hyperkalemia
###### Valsartan 缬沙坦
- t max 2-4 hr, t 1/2 9 hr
- Protein binding 65%
- The antihypertension effect lasts 24 hr
- Excrete by kidney and feces without metabolise
- Usage: hypertension, heart failure
- Dizziness, headache, fatigue
###### Irbesartan 厄贝沙坦
- Potent and long-lasting AT1 blocker (high selectivity)
- t max 1-2 hr and last for 24 hr
- bioavailability 60-80%, 90% protein binding
- t 1/2 11-15 hr
- Mainly metabolized by liver, partly excreted by kidney and feces
- Usage: hypertension, 能减轻肾损害，减少尿蛋白， 增加肌酐清除率
###### Candesartan 坎地沙坦
- an active metabolite
- Candesartan cilexetil（坎地沙坦酯） quickly hydrolyzed to candesartan
- Potent, long-lasting and highly selective AT1 blocker
- bioavailability 42%, 99.5% binding
- t 1/2 3-11 hr
- Excreted by kidney and bile acid
- Usage: hypertension, chronic heart failure
###### Allisartan isoproxil 阿利沙坦酯
- Prodrug, → EXP 3174 (also an active metabolites of losartan)
- 是我国第一个自主研发的AT1 受体阻断药
- bioavailability 60%, >99.7% protein binding
- 化学结构的改变使阿利沙坦酯可不经肝脏 CYP450 酶代谢，而是通过消化道脂酶进行水解 ，从而显著降低高血压患者的肝脏负担
- 主要经消化道排出，少量经肾脏排出


# 4. Angiotensin receptor neprilysin inhibitor, ARNI（血管紧张素受体-脑啡肽酶抑制药）
###### Sacubitril valsartan sodium, 沙库巴曲缬沙坦钠
- 口服后分解为沙库巴曲（进一步在肝脏经羧酸酯酶代谢为 LBQ657）和缬沙坦
- 口服生物利用度 60%，血浆蛋白结合率94-97%
- 药物通过肝、肾双通道排泄
- 多靶点协同产生排钠利尿、舒张血管和防治心血管重构的作用
- 适用于EF降低的慢性心力衰竭患者， 可降低心血管死亡和心 力衰竭的住院风险
- 高血压，提高高血压合并肥胖以及高血压合并肾功能不全患者 的外周胰岛素敏感性，增加脂肪动员。
- 不良反应：血管性水肿、低血压、肾功能损害和高钾血症
- 禁止与ACEI合用，停止ACEI后36小时才可以开始使用
