# 1 Lung infectious pneumonia

### Bacterial Pneumonia

#### 1. Lobar pneumonia 大叶性肺炎
[[BMS1_Pathology.pdf#page=12&selection=124,0,125,17|Pathology of BMS1_病理_zsy_net, 页面 12]]
- *def*. an acute bacterial infection resulting in fibrinous inflammation of a large portion of a lobe or of an entire lobe
- pathogen: S.pneumoniae (>90%) 肺炎链球菌
- pathogenesis:
	1. bacteria
	2. alveoli
	3. grow and multiply
	4. allergic reaction, capillary dilation, serious exudates
	5. Kohn's pores
	6. spreading entire lobe
- clinical course:
	- abrupt onset of high fever, shaking chills, pleuritic chest pain
	- cough productive of mucopurulent sputum, (brown sputum, "rusty sputum"铁锈色痰)
- ==morphology==

#### 2. Lobular pneumonia/ Bronchopneumonia 小叶性肺炎/支气管肺炎
[[BMS1_Pathology.pdf#page=13&selection=27,1,31,18|Pathology of BMS1_病理_zsy_net, 页面 13]]
- conception: acute purulent inflammation
- etiology: almost any organism; often mixed infection
	- *hypostatic pneumonia*：pulmonary edema from cardiac failure
	- *aspiration pneumonia*：patients in coma or heavy anesthesia
- morphology:
	- grossly
		- patchy distribution of inflammation that generally involves more than one lobe; frequently bilateral and basal
		- initial: bronchi and bronchioles, with extensions into the adjacent alveoli
	- histologically
		- suppurative exudates that fills the bronchi......
		- immediately surrounding areas of consolidation
		- large intervening areas
	- clinically
		- high fever, cough with sputum
		- moist rales in both lungs
		- X ray focal shadow 灶性阴影

#### 3. Interstitial pneumonia 间质性肺炎
[[BMS1_Pathology.pdf#page=13&selection=172,1,174,24|Pathology of BMS1_病理_zsy_net, 页面 13]]
- *def*. an exudative inflammation involving the alveolar septa and pulmonary interstitium
- etiology: the common agents are viruses and mycoplasma
- morphology:
	- Grossly:
		- 病变不明显
		- affected areas are congested, volume slightly enlarge
		- moderate elevation of WBC count
	- LM:
		- inflammatory reaction within the alveolar septa 肺泡间隔 （增宽-水肿、充血、炎细胞浸润）
		- virus infection: inclusion bodies 包涵体
		- [[3 CRBU1 呼吸系统疾病病理-2.pdf#page=5&selection=0,0,0,16|3 CRBU1 呼吸系统疾病病理-2, 页面 5]]
		- [[3 CRBU1 呼吸系统疾病病理-2.pdf#page=6&selection=0,0,1,0|3 CRBU1 呼吸系统疾病病理-2, 页面 6]]
- clinical features:
	- extremely varied
	- onset: acute, nonspecific febrile illness
	- fever, headache, malaise, cough with minimal sputum
	- prognosis is good

# 2 COPD 慢性阻塞性肺病
[[BMS1_Pathology.pdf#page=13&selection=276,0,277,6|Pathology of BMS1_病理_zsy_net, 页面 13]]
- chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, 是一组肺实质与气道受到病理损害后，导致以气道持久的完全和不完全的阻塞、呼气阻力增加、肺功能不全为共同特征的肺疾病。主要包括：
#### 1. chronic bronchitis 慢性支气管炎
- 慢性非特异性炎；
- 诊断标准：反复发作咳嗽咳痰或伴有喘鸣音。每年持续3个月、连续2年
- etiology and pathogenesis:
	- infection
	- smoking
	- air pollution and allergy
	- intrinsic factors
- 病变常起于较大支气管，各级支气管可受累
- ==morphology==
	- grossly
		- bronchial wall is thickened, hyperemic and edematous
		- lumina of the bronchi and bronchioles may contain mucus plugs 粘液栓 purulent exudate 脓性分泌物
	- histologically: 粘液腺增生肥大，黏膜上皮杯状细胞化生
- clinically: variable
	- productive cough without ventilatory dysfunction
	- develop COPD with significant outflow obstruction marked by:
		*Hypercapnia(血碳酸过多症）, hypoxemia(低氧血症 ), and cyanosis（发绀）*
- complications: 肺气肿，肺心病，支气管扩张，支气管肺炎。肺癌

#### 2. pulmonary emphysema 肺气肿
- *def*.  abnormal permanent enlargement of the air space to the terminal bronchiole accompanied by destruction of their walls without significant fibrosis 终末支气管；含气量增加，肺组织体积膨大
- etiology and pathogenesis
	1. brochitis (obstruction) 支气管阻塞性通气功能障碍
	2. α1-antitrypsin deficiency 弹性蛋白酶及其抑制物失衡
	3. smoking  吸烟
- classification:
	1. **alveolar emphysema** [[3 CRBU1 呼吸系统疾病病理-2.pdf#page=37&selection=0,0,0,18|3 CRBU1 呼吸系统疾病病理-2, 页面 37]]
	2. **interstitial** **emphysema**
		- 肺内压急剧增高（肋骨骨折、胸壁穿透伤或剧烈咳嗽等）导致细支气管或肺泡间隔破裂，空气进入肺间质
		- 桶状胸（barrel-shaped thorax）
		- 呼气性呼吸困难；自发性气胸
	3. others: 肺大疱
---
#### 3. bronchiectasis 支气管扩张症
- *def*, permanent dilatation of bronchi and bronchiole due to destruction of the muscle and elastic supporting tissue
- cough; purulent sputum
- 分泌物潴留继发化脓性炎症；咳嗽，大量脓痰
- morphology: largely dilated airways: cystic, cylindrical
- clinicopathological correlation:
	- postural coughing with largely quantity of purulent sputum, fetid sputum
	- hemoptysis 咯血
	- cor pulmonale 肺心病 （肺动脉高压，引起慢性肺源性心脏病-右心）
- 并发症：肺脓肿、继发败血症、肺纤维化
---
#### 4. bronchial asthma 支气管哮喘
- 气管可逆性痉挛；阵发性；过敏性炎症
- clinically, 反复发作性喘息，呼吸性呼吸困难伴喘鸣音

# 3. Pneumoconiosis 尘肺
[[BMS1_Pathology.pdf#page=16&selection=4,0,8,16|Pathology of BMS1_病理_zsy_net, 页面 16]]
- 以粉尘结节和肺纤维化为主要病变的常见职业病
- 硅肺：游离的二氧化硅粉尘微粒在肺内蓄积引起的以硅结节形成及肺间质广泛纤维化为主要病变的全身性疾病
- etiology and pathogenesis:
	- Silica particles (<5 μm ) enter lungs and are phagocytozed by macrophages.
	- 巨噬细胞聚集--肉芽肿性疾病（硅结节）
- morphology:
	- formation of silicitic nodules
	- diffuse fibrosis of pulmonary stroma
	- [[3 CRBU1 呼吸系统疾病病理-2.pdf#page=65&selection=34,0,34,10|3 CRBU1 呼吸系统疾病病理-2, 页面 65]]
- complications: 结核，肺心病，肺部感染，自发性气胸

# 5. lung cancer
[[BMS1_Pathology.pdf#page=16&selection=350,0,352,36|Pathology of BMS1_病理_zsy_net, 页面 16]]
- 死因第一位
- etiology:
	- smoking
	- air pollution
	- occupation
	- accumulation of genetic changes
- morphology
	- **central type**: arises in the lining epithelium of major bronchi, usually close to the hilus of the lung
	- **peripheral type**: arises from the small bronchi
	- **diffuse type**: arises from terminal pulmonary tissue
