## **Schizophrenia 精神分裂症**
#### Signs & Symptoms
##### Positive symptoms（I型，阳性症状）
- Delusions (妄想) - fixed false belief outside cultural norm (bizarre vs. non bizarre)
- Hallucinations (幻觉) – 幻听、幻视
- Disorganization (思维紊乱) - pattern of speech or behavior, making up words without a meaning (neologisms) ——滔滔不绝，毫无逻辑
##### Negative symptoms（II型，阴性症状）
- Affective flattening（情感淡漠）
- Avolition / Amotivation (decreased motivation)动机缺乏
- Autistic(孤独) behaviors (social withdrawal )
- Anhedonia (inability to experience pleasure )无快感
- Ambivalence (coexistence of opposing attitudes or feelings，矛盾心态)
- Anosognosia (疾病感缺失) (impaired awareness of illness )

- Drugs inhibit DA synthesis or storage or antagonize DA receptor ameliorate schizophrenia
- Increase intensity of D 2 receptor can be found in the brain of schizophrenia patient

## Antipsychotic drugs
- 药物能够减轻精神分裂症的症状，但同时也有各种不良反应
- 抗精神分裂症药不仅能够用于精神分裂症的治疗，还能够用于其他 一些疾病的治疗，如躁狂症
- **抗精神分裂症药物只能缓解症状，不能治愈疾病**

**经典**： Phenothiazines（吩噻嗪类）、 Thioxanthenes (硫杂蒽类)、 Butyrophenones（丁酰苯类）；==Typical antipsychotic drugs are dopamine D2 receptor antagonists==
**非经典**：
- Clozapine 氯氮平 Blocking D 4 and 5-HT receptors
- Risperidone 利培酮 Blocking D 2 and 5-HT2 receptors
- Ziprasidone 齐拉西酮 Blocking D2 , D3 and 5-HT2A, 5-HT2c, 5-HT1A, 5- HT1D, α receptors
- Aripiprazole 阿立哌唑 Blocking D2 , D3 and 5-HT2A, 5-HT1A receptors receptors

高效价/低剂量：锥体外系作用明显  
低效价/高剂量：锥体外系副作用较轻

# Phenothiazines（吩噻嗪类）
###### Chlorpromazine 氯丙嗪  (Wintermine 冬眠灵)
1. **Pharmacokinetics**
	- Absorption: slow (2-4 h to steady state) and irregular
	- High lipophilic; Lasted for long time; Individual
	- Distribution: wildly, brain/serum = 10/1
	- Metabolism: P450
	- Excretion: Kidney
2. **Pharmacological effects**
	1. ==Central==: Blocking central D2 dopamine receptors
		1. **Antipsychotic effects (neuroleptic effects 神经安定作用)**
			- For the treatment of schizophrenia
			- Control the excitation and hallucinations (take weeks to months)
			- 正常人服用：安静、活动减少、感情淡漠、注意力下 降、兴趣降低、答话迟缓等
			- 对抑郁无效，甚至可能加剧
		2. **Antiemetic effects(镇吐作用)**
			- Inhibition the chemoreceptor trigger zone (CTZ) dopaminergic function
			- 对前庭刺激引起的呕吐无效
			- 对顽固性呃逆有效
		3. **Poikilothermic effects (体温调节作用)：下丘脑体温调节中枢**
			- Hypothermic anesthesia
			- Artificial hibernation (人工冬眠)，还包括异丙嗪、哌替啶
		4. **Extrapyramidal effects**
			- 抑制黑质-纹状体通路的D2受体
			- Primary adverse effects
		5. **Potentiating the effects of central depressants**
			- Sedative-hypnotics（易唤醒，神志清楚）
			- General anesthetics, ethanol
		6. **Endocrine effects: 结节-漏斗通路**
			- Prolactin ↑
			- ACTH, growth hormone ↓
	2. ==Autonomic nervous system==:
		1. **Hypotensive effects**:α receptor blockade, postural hypotension
		2. **Anticholinergic effects (M receptor blockade)**:Dry mouth, constipation, blurred vision, urinary retention, etc
3. **Clinical uses**
	1. **Schizophrenia**:inhibiting the positive symptoms 各种原因引起的兴奋、幻觉、妄想等
	2. **Emesis and hiccough**:used for emesis （呕吐） and hiccough（呃逆） but ineffective on motion sickness
	3. **Hypothermic anesthesia（低温麻醉）**: (combined with lowering room temperature) and artificial hibernation (combined with Pethidine 哌替啶 and promethazine异丙嗪)
4. **Adverse effects**
	1. *Side effects*
		1. Central depression: 嗜睡、淡漠、无力
		2. Peripheral effects: *postural hypotension*, dry mouth, and other effects resulting from muscarinic and α receptor blockade
	2. *锥体外系作用*
		1. **Due to DA receptor block:**
			a) Parkinsonism (帕金森综合征)
			b) Akathisia (静坐不能) 
			c) Acute dystonia (急性肌张力障碍)
		2. **Due to supersensitive to DA:** Tardive dyskinesia (迟发性运动障碍)：口面部不自主的刻 板运动，舞蹈样手足徐动症。不能被M受体阻断剂缓解
		3. **Other central reactions**
			- Neuroleptic maglinant syndrome (神经阻滞药恶 性综合征）表现为难以控制的锥体外束和延髓损害症状
			- Psychotic reactions (药源性精神异常)：意识障碍、兴 奋、躁动、抑郁、幻觉、妄想等
			- Epilepsy and convulsion (癫痫和惊厥) : lowering seizure threshold
		4. **Allergic and hemological reactions**) :skin reactions, leukopenia, obstructive jaundice, liver damage
		5. **CVS reactions**:
			- Arrhythmia
			- Hypotension: treated by α receptor agonists
			- Sudden death (esp. aged patients with CVS diseases)
		6. **Endocrine reactions**:
			- Hyperplasia of mammary glands (乳腺增生), galactorrhea (溢乳), amenorrhea (闭经 )
			- Child growth retard(生长抑制)
		7. **Acute intoxication**: Severe CNS depression, coma, severe hypotension
		8. **Contraindications**: Epilepsy；Coma；Aged patients with CVS disorders； Severe hepatic and renal dysfunction
		9. **Drug-drug interactions**：
			- Increase the central depression
				- morphine（吗啡） and pethidine（哌替啶 ） induced respiratory depression and hypopressure.
			- Decrease the effects of L-DOPA
			- The metabolism can be increased by P450 inducer
